Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

What are data credits and how do they work?

Data credits are used for enriching prospect profiles with emails, phone numbers, and other contact information. Each credit enriches one contact. Credits reset monthly and unused credits don't roll over.

What happens if I exceed my credit limit?

If you exceed your monthly credit limit, you can purchase additional credit packs or upgrade to a higher plan. We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit.

Can I mix different plans for my team?

Currently, all team members must be on the same plan. However, you can add individual seats to Team plans and customize Agency plans to fit your specific needs.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with 100 data credits and access to all core features. No credit card required to start.

Can I change or cancel my plan anytime?

Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle, and we provide prorated refunds for downgrades.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes! Annual billing saves you 25% compared to monthly billing. You'll see the discounted pricing when you toggle to annual billing above.
