Our Story

From one recruiter's pain-point to an AI platform for thousands.

It Started at Raydar

In 2023, our founder Sarah was running a boutique recruiting firm called Raydar. Despite having great relationships and industry expertise, she was drowning in manual tasks—researching prospects, crafting personalized outreach, and tracking endless follow-ups.

After missing out on a major client because a competitor could move faster, Sarah realized the problem wasn't talent or strategy. It was time. She needed to automate the grunt work so she could focus on what humans do best: building relationships and closing deals.

That's when Quinton AI was born—not as a tech experiment, but as a solution to a real recruiter's daily struggle.
