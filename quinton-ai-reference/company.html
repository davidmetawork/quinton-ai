About Quinton AI

Building the operating system for modern recruiters.

Our Mission

Help agency recruiters sign more clients and place more candidates by automating the tedious work that keeps them from building relationships and closing deals.

Our Vision

Become the AI operating system for recruiting firms, powering every aspect of talent acquisition from sourcing to placement.

Core Values

The principles that guide how we build and grow

Automation with Empathy

We automate the mundane so humans can focus on what they do best: building meaningful relationships and making great matches.

Radical Transparency

We believe in open communication, clear pricing, and honest feedback. No hidden fees, no black boxes, no surprises.

Customer > Feature

We prioritize solving real customer problems over building flashy features. Every decision starts with "How does this help our users succeed?"
